…and have a strong passion for making a positive impact. I have been visually designing digital products and websites to empower the user for just over four years. Currently crafting an inclusive and adaptive time management tool in FinTech.

I began as a Marketing Consultant and designed my way into a Visual Designer role after years of perseverance, dedication, and passion. I’m mostly self-taught in graphics - watching countless tutorials and excelled through an accelerated certification program through UC Berkeley in UX Design.

It doesn’t matter what industry I am in, I enjoy all aspects of design! I have worked for a broad spectrum of companies within Retail, Insurance, Print Management, Food & Beverage, Commercial Real Estate and Financial Technology.
